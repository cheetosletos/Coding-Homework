KORO'S BIG DAY OUT

Schedule

  • Nov 2nd - Nov 30th

Koro's Big Day Out is about Koro the puppy, who runs away from his mistress, experiences some adventures around town and who is finally happily returned home.

Run Time: 15 min

THE DAY I BOUGHT A STAR

Schedule

  • Dec 1st - Dec 26th

A young boy is tired of the city and escapes into the country. Two strangers trade him a strange seed. The boy accepts and the seed sprouts into a miniature planet, which continues to grow.

Run Time: 16 min

THE WHALE HUNT

Schedule

  • Jan 3rd 2017 - Jan 30th 2017

A story about school children pretending they are building a boat. As imagination replaces reality, they find themselves on the ocean, hunting for a whale. A big, gentle whale appears, accompanies them back to land and plays with them. Then the fantasy ends and the children are back in their class room.

Run Time: 16 min
